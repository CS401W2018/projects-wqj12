Family Picture

  • The Story of Hua Mulan This story takes place in ancient China.
  • In order to prevent her elderly father from going to the battlefield again, a woman chooses to pretend to be her father and join the army.
  • Actually, I don't know his mother's name, so I'll call him Mrs. Hua for now.

Each characters

Mulan

Hua Mulan has been very filial since childhood. He has an older sister and a younger brother, and has a harmonious relationship with his brothers and sisters. However, his family environment made him strong-willed. He did not think that women were weaker than men, and would help the family with important things. So in the end, he joined the war in place of his father without regrets.

Father

His father had served his country when he was young and made many contributions to his country, but the war also caused him to suffer various injuries, which led to his poor health in old age. When the country needed him again, he was no longer able to fight again.

Mother

Hua Mulan's mother was also a noble lady from a prominent family with a good family environment, so her mother's character became very virtuous. She worked hard to raise three children at home and had a very good relationship with her husband. She was willing to support any decision her husband made. It was precisely because of this character that when Hua Mulan argued for her father, her mother was also very worried.
